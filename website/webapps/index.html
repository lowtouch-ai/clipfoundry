How It Works

Your Video Producer, On Autopilot

Five steps from raw idea to finished MP4, fully orchestrated by AI agents.

01

Send Your Idea

Drop a prompt via email or chat — a topic, a brand name, a script concept. No templates, no forms.

→
02

AI Researches & Scripts

Gemini researches the topic, assigns a persona, and writes a punchy, paced script optimised for video models.

→
03

Review & Approve

Read the proposed script. Reply with feedback to iterate, or say "Looks good" to trigger production.

→
04

AI Generates Clips

Airflow splits the script into scenes, generates video segments using AI models, and stitches them together.

→
05

Receive Your Video

Get a secure download link for your finished MP4 — delivered via email or directly in the chat interface.

Two Workflows

Choose How You Create

Whether you want a hands-off producer relationship or instant results from a studio interface — ClipFoundry has you covered.

Email

The Conversational Producer

Best for: Refining ideas, script iteration, and hands-off research.

  • → Send a minimal prompt email with your idea
  • → Agent detects format, researches, writes script
  • → Receive script proposal and iterate via reply
  • → Approve with "Looks good" to start production
  • → Receive finished MP4 download link by email
Example prompt

"Create me a reel on NVIDIA CEO's recent comment on the five layers of AI."

Chat

The Fire-and-Go Studio

Best for: Quick generation, using specific images, and instant previews.

  • → Open the OpenWebUI chat interface
  • → Upload images (optional) and enter your prompt
  • → Agent splits script, generates clips per scene
  • → Segments stitched into a final MP4 automatically
  • → Preview and download directly in the chat window
Example prompt

"Create a 20s energetic promo for a new coffee brand called Morning Jolt using these images."

Features

Everything a Video Studio Needs

Built for production environments — not just demos.

AI Script Generation

Gemini researches topics and writes influencer-optimised scripts with persona assignment and timing built in.

AI Video Synthesis

Integrates with Google Veo and Nano Banana for high-quality video segment generation from text and images.

Image-to-Video

Upload your brand assets — logos, product shots, backgrounds — and ClipFoundry animates them into clips.

Scene Splitting & Stitching

Scripts are automatically split into optimally-timed scenes, generated independently, and merged into a single cohesive video.

Email-Driven Workflow

No dashboards required. The entire production cycle — from idea submission to delivery — runs over email.

PG-13 Content Filter

Built-in negative prompt filtering and content safety checks keep generated video brand-safe and compliant.

Iterative Feedback Loop

Not happy with the first script? Reply with notes. The agent rewrites and resubmits until you approve.

Self-Hosted Appliance

Runs entirely on your own infrastructure. Full privacy, no cloud lock-in, complete control over your assets.

Under the Hood

Powered by Best-in-Class Components

A self-contained stack built for reliability, privacy, and scale.

Apache Airflow

Orchestrates the entire production pipeline — inbox monitoring, script generation, video DAGs.

Google Gemini

Handles intent classification, topic research, persona assignment, and script writing.

Google Veo & Nano Banana

State-of-the-art AI video generation models producing high-quality clips from scene descriptions.

Shared Storage (MinIO)

Assets are securely passed between agents and production stages via a shared storage layer.

Open WebUI

Chat interface for the Fire-and-Go Studio workflow — upload assets, enter prompts, preview results.

Docker / Self-Hosted

Entire stack ships as Docker containers — deploy on your infrastructure with full data sovereignty.
